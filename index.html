About

Anders is a passionate and curious developer who has decided to focus himself on development for the front end of websites. Over the last five years he has honed his craft. His HTML is semantically decisive and succinct as well as both expressive and eloquent. His Javascript is both sensible and creative, approaching problems by making the best use of his ever expanding knowledge. Anders has been commended on both the breadth and specificity of his CSS skills.

The combination of these three skills mean that he is capable of creating interesting and unusual, on brand and responsive websites which are both accessible and aesthetically pleasing. Anders is constantly reading and researching in his field, embracing the best new techniques and discarding those which are merely fads – this means that he will always strive to keep his work at the cutting edge of web development.

Availability

Based in Suffolk, Anders is available to work in and around East Anglia, providing work on a contract/freelance basis. He is available to work remotely throughout Britain and Europe. If your company is interested in creating excellent products and services with a focus on quality, please contact Anders via email.

Projects

FESuffolk

Anders has organised a front end based user group in his home town of Ipswich in suffolk. Every month developers and designers meet to talk tech and share presentations based on front end development and design. These meet-ups have helped with the cross pollination of ideas and the understanding of new development concepts.
